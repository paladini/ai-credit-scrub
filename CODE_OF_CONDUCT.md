# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, or background.

## Expected behavior

- Use welcoming and respectful language.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Focus discussion on ideas, evidence, and the project.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, and publishing private
information without permission are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the maintainer at fnpaladini@gmail.com. Reports
will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
